Within this mission, we collaborate with major hospitals in Ulaanbaatar such as the National Cancer Center, National Center for Communicable Diseases, Brilliant, Theriomed, and South District General Hospital. With the latest technological equipment and a highly experienced, friendly medical team, we continue to expand and implement advanced diagnostic services.
